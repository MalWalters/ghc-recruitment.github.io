Who we are

We are a firm of consultants committed to providing a tailored and quality centric service. We are focussed on accessing human talent across the Banking/Finance; Projects; Accounting/Tax; Technology and Utilities sectors. Our company culture is driven by providing both our clients and candidates with a premium service that exceeds their expectations. Our assignments are tailored to your specific requirements. We differentiate from our competitors by creating a customised, transparent and well engaged process for both our clients and candidates. We partner with our clients to ensure that we proactive and responsive to your needs.

GHC prides itself on being at the forefront of market and staffing trends. We incorporate the “human element” in all that we do whilst ensuring that our solutions are effective and long term.

At GHC we understand that our clients have complex staffing requirements and our candidates may need a customised approach with their employment search

At GHC, we offer:

  • Global player with access to candidates locally and offshore
  • Client experience covering Banking; Finance; Insurance; Accounting/Tax ; Utilities and Technology
  • Highly experienced consultants with extensive commercial knowledge

Our operating model is carefully crafted and managed to deliver client led outcomes. Our relationship with our clients is driven by the feedback we receive . We value our clients and service our customers and candidates alike.
