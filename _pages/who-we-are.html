Who we are

We are an innovative and well governed firm committed to providing a professional and quality centric service. GHC is focussed on accessing high calibre talent across the Banking/Finance; Change Management; Projects; Accounting/Tax; Utilities and I.T sectors. Our assignments are tailored to you and your business needs. At GHC we create a customised, and well engaged process for both our clients and candidates. We partner with you to ensure that we are proactive and responsive to your requirements.

At GHC we understand that our clients have complex staffing requirements and that our candidates may need a customised approach with their employment search.

At GHC, we offer:

  • Consultants with extensive multi discipline experience (domestic and global experience)
  • Global player with access to candidates locally and offshore
  • Client experience covering Banking; Finance; Insurance; Accounting/Tax ; Utilities and Technology
  • Highly experienced consultants with extensive commercial knowledge
